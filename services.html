Here's how we can help

  • Bathroom, Kitchen and Floor Caulking

    These areas are often exposed to water. Quality joint sealing is therefore critical in preventing water damage to underlying building materials and also stops grime and bugs getting underneath.

  • Articulation Caulking

    Articulation joints allow for movement between brittle materials such as bricks. Effective sealing therefore requires flexibility to allow for such movements whilst maintaining a water tight seal against the elements.

  • Fire Rated Joints

    Fire rated joints are an essential safety measure and must meet requirements set out by the Building Code of Australia. Correct product selection and application are therefore critical to ensure compliance.

  • Concrete Joints

    Concrete is a brittle material and so concrete joints are used to allow for expansion and contraction without cracking. It is therefore important to have a seal that is flexible but also robust in preventing water ingress.

  • Precast Panels

    Precast panel joint sealing plays a vital part in maintaining the integrity of the external envelope of a building. A sealing solution must prevent the ingress of water and air and also withstand long-term UV exposure.

  • Internal Waterproofing

    Waterproofing is a fundamental requirement of construction. It is therefore critical to select and apply the membrane or coating correctly, to prevent water damage and protect the building's structural integrity.
